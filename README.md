## Core Methodology: The Forge Cycle 🔄

ReflexForge operates on a proprietary four-phase loop, inspired by principles of deliberate practice and spaced retrieval.

1.  **Extract (The Ingestion Phase):** The agentic tool-chain ingests content from any source (video, audio, text, image). It identifies the core cognitive principle, logical fallacy, mental model, or decision heuristic being presented.
2.  **Distill (The Compression Phase):** The principle is compressed into a *Reflex Byte*—a minimal, actionable cognitive trigger. This removes all fluff, context noise, and narrative bias.
3.  **Challenge (The Adversarial Phase):** The AI generates contextualized micro-scenarios that force you to apply the Reflex Byte under simulated pressure (time constraints, conflicting information, emotional triggers).
4.  **Solidify (The Consolidation Phase):** Successful passes are scheduled into an exponential spaced repetition algorithm. Failures trigger an immediate re-teaching loop from a different angle (multilingual support for encoding variation).

This runs entirely offline on your device via a PWA and a lightweight Android wrapper. Your data, your reflexes.

## Key Features ✨

### 🏋️ Multi-Source Reflex Ingestion
Just as IronClaw let you import a recipe from any URL, ReflexForge lets you import a mental model from any media. Drop a link from YouTube, an Instagram caption, a PDF of a philosophy paper, or a photo of a whiteboard diagram. The AI extracts the cognitive payload.

### 🤖 Full Agentic Tool-Calling (Offline)
Your personal trainer is a swarm of local AI agents. One agent reads the source, another constructs the scenario, another tracks your emotional state via text sentiment. No cloud dependency. All decisions happen on your metal.

### 📈 Adaptive Difficulty & Micro-Habits
The system never repeats the same scenario. It learns your weak points (e.g., patience under time pressure, logical fallacies in reciprocity) and constructs custom drills. Build micro-habits in 5-minute sessions that integrate seamlessly into your workflow.

### 🔄 Offline-First Sync & Cross-Platform
All training data is stored locally. When online, your progress matrix syncs silently across devices. The Android wrapper delivers native notification nudges to "forge a reflex" during your commute.

### 🌐 Multilingual Cognitive Encoding
Learning a new mindset? The system can present the same Reflex Byte in different languages to deepen semantic encoding. Train your reflexes in English, Spanish, French, German, or Mandarin—whichever best unlocks the insight.

### 🧩 Responsive UI – Any Screen, Any State
The interface adapts from a full dashboard on desktop to a single-tap "reflex trigger" screen on your phone. Dark mode, high contrast, and reading-mode optimized for low-light sessions.

### 📜 Complete Privacy & Data Sovereignty
Your cognitive journey is yours alone. No servers know your weak spots. No analytics track your hesitation patterns. The license ensures the source code remains open for audit, but your personal training data never leaves your device.

### 🤝 24/7 Agentic Support Drills
Feeling stuck at 3 AM? The AI doesn't sleep. You can request an immediate "adversarial sparring session" on any previously imported concept. It will generate fresh, aggressive scenarios to test your resolve.

## How It Works: A Walkthrough 🚶

1.  **Discover:** You find a profound quote from a video essay or a powerful mental model from a web article.
2.  **Ingest:** Press the floating action button, paste the link or upload the image. A progress indicator shows the AI extracting the cognitive essence.

6.  **Repeat & Evolve:** Over weeks, your unconscious reactions sharpen. You find yourself responding to stress with pre-forged patterns of clarity.

## Architecture Overview 🏛️

ReflexForge is built on a modular, event-driven architecture:

- **Ingestion Agent:** Handles multi-format parsing (YouTube transcript API, OCR for images, HTML scraper for web).
- **Reflex Compiler:** Transforms extracted concepts into structured Reflex Byte objects (JSON schema).
- **Scenario Generator:** Uses a local inference engine (ONNX runtime) to generate adversarial scenarios. No cloud API calls.
- **Spaced Repetition Engine:** A custom scheduler based on SM-2 with emotional state weighting.
- **PWA Shell:** Service workers for offline caching. Sync adapter for optional cross-device synchronization.
- **Android Wrapper:** Allows native push notifications and background training reminders.
